Christos

The floating action button can fling out related actions upon press.

The button should remain on screen after the menu is invoked. Tapping in the same spot should either activate the most commonly used action or close the open menu.

  • EXAMPLE
  • HTML 
 
<md-button ng-class="[vm.buttonClass, vm.buttonHue]" aria-label="flat button">Button</md-button>
<md-select placeholder="Choose a style" ng-model="vm.buttonClass"></md-option>
   <md-option value="md-primary">Primary </md-option>
   <md-option value="md-primary">Accent</md-option>
   <md-option value="md-primary">Warn </md-option>
</md-button>
